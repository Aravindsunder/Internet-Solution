Additional Information

FC Barcelona, founded in 1899, is one of the most successful and historic football clubs in the world. Based in Barcelona, Spain, the club is known for its attacking philosophy, youth development, and global fanbase. Their motto, "Més que un club" (More than a club), reflects their cultural and social influence beyond football.

In recent years, Barcelona has faced financial struggles, leading to the shocking departure of Messi in 2021. However, under manager Xavi Hernández, the club has focused on rebuilding with young talents like Pedri, Gavi, and Lamine Yamal, winning La Liga in 2022–23.
